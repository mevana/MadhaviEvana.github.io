About Me...

I am Madhavi Evana, a first year Master's student in Data Analytics with major in Computer Science at George Washington University located in the Capital city of Washington DC.

I am a Data Scientist who enjoys looking for data-driven solutions and is passionate about implementing machine learning and deep learning to create solutions that are pragmatic and easy to interpret. I am motivated by the belief that data can be used to make better decisions. I want to be able to make it easier for people to understand code and use data efficiently. I primarily use Python, but also has experience with R and Java.

As a part of my curriculum, I have collaborated on several projects and worked on real-time, social media,and environmental data.I also presented results to technical and non-technical audiences..
